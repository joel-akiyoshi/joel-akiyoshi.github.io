Crispy Tofu

Ingredients

  • 1 block tofu, extra firm
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Sauce Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp barbeque sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Slice the tofu into 1 inch cubes.
  3. Coat the tofu with cornstarch.
  4. Fry the tofu in a large pan until golden brown (12 minutes each side).
  5. Mix together sauce ingredients, drizzle over the top as desired.
  6. Serve over rice.

Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 package noodles (any type), dried or fresh
  • 1 jarred sauce OR sauce packet (sometimes you have to add a can of diced tomatoes or tomato paste if you use the sauce packet, read it first)
  • 1 pkg ground turkey (or sausage; see optional ingredients)
  • 1 onion (chopped up) or onion flakes

Optional Ingredients

  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Bell pepper
  • kielbasa sausage
  • fresh garlic or garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles per directions on packaging in stockpot, at least ⅔ full of water. You can salt the water but don’t add any oil. Drain in colander in cool water and set aside.
  2. Using the same stockpot, brown your meat in a little bit of oil. You can also add the onions and cook them until the meat is no longer pink and the onions are translucent. Drain your oil.
  3. Add any other optional veggies to cook them before adding your sauce.

Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 1 package ground meat (turkey, beef, etc)
  • ½ cup dry breadcrumbs (or stuffing mix)
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (or equivalent onion flakes)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper

Glaze Ingredients

  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 2 Tablespoons brown sugar
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg (this is optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine all meatloaf ingredients in a mixing bowl
  3. Put meatloaf in the pan, scraping the bowl with a spatula to get it all in evenly.
  4. Make the glaze in a smaller bowl, then pour over the top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake for 1 hour and 15 miniutes.
